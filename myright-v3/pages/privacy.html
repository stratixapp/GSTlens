Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2025

MyRight ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our services.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account data: Name, email address, and authentication details when you sign up.
  • Document data: Information you provide when generating legal documents (parties, addresses, amounts).
  • Usage data: Pages visited, documents generated, and app interaction logs.
  • Payment data: Transaction IDs from Razorpay (we never store card numbers).

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To generate and store your legal documents
  • To provide customer support and account management
  • To process payments securely via Razorpay
  • To send service-related emails (document ready, receipts)
  • To improve our AI models and document templates

3. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely on Google Firebase (Firestore) servers located in Asia. We use industry-standard 256-bit SSL encryption for all data in transit. Your documents are never shared with third parties without your explicit consent.

4. Guest Mode

If you use MyRight as a Guest (without creating an account), no personal data is stored on our servers. Generated documents remain only in your browser session and are not saved to our database.

5. Third-Party Services

  • Google Firebase — Authentication and database storage
  • Anthropic Claude AI — Document generation
  • Razorpay — Payment processing
  • Google Fonts — Typography

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data at any time. You can delete your account from the Profile page, which will permanently remove all your data from our systems.

7. Cookies

We use minimal cookies required for authentication (Firebase auth tokens). We do not use advertising cookies or track you across other websites.

8. Contact Us

For privacy-related queries, contact us at privacy@myright.app
