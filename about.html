About GST Lens
India's smartest AI-powered GST invoice scanner
built for small businesses, shop owners & CA firms

💡 What is GST Lens?

GST Lens is a Progressive Web App (PWA) built by Stratix Technologies that helps Indian businesses manage their GST invoices smarter. Simply scan or upload any GST invoice — our AI extracts all the tax data in seconds.

No manual data entry. No spreadsheets. No missed deductions. GST Lens handles the heavy lifting so you can focus on running your business.

🇮🇳 Built specifically for India's GST ecosystem — supporting CGST, SGST, IGST, HSN codes, GSTR-1, GSTR-2A and GSTR-3B filing summaries.
