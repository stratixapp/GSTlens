📱 2. Description of Service

GST Lens by Stratix is a free Progressive Web Application (PWA) for Indian businesses providing:

  • On-device invoice scanning using Tesseract OCR — no data sent to external servers
  • Built-in GST Intelligence Engine for offline, rule-based tax analysis
  • GSTR-1, GSTR-2A, and GSTR-3B report generation
  • ITC (Input Tax Credit) tracking and vendor management
  • PDF, CSV, and Excel export of bill data
  • Optional Firebase cloud sync for multi-device access

All invoice processing occurs entirely on your device. No invoice images or extracted data are transmitted to any external AI service or third-party API.
