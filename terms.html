🔍 2. Description of Service

GST Lens by Stratix is an AI-powered GST invoice analysis tool that allows users to:

  • Scan and extract data from GST invoices using OCR
  • Automatically calculate CGST, SGST and IGST amounts
  • Generate GSTR-1, GSTR-2A and GSTR-3B summaries
  • Export reports as PDF, Excel and CSV
  • Store invoice history in the cloud
