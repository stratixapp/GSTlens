📋 1. Information We Collect

Account Information

When you register or sign in, we collect your name, email address, and optionally your GSTIN (GST Identification Number). This is used solely to identify your account and personalise your experience.

Invoice Data

When you scan or upload an invoice, we extract and store the following structured data:

  • Vendor name and GSTIN
  • Invoice number and date
  • CGST, SGST, IGST amounts
  • HSN/SAC codes
  • Taxable value and total amount
📄 Uploaded invoice images and PDFs are processed locally on your device using OCR (Optical Character Recognition) and optionally via Google Gemini AI for improved accuracy. Images may be stored in Firebase Storage linked to your account for reference.

Usage Data

We collect anonymised usage data such as scan count and feature usage to improve the app. We do not collect device identifiers or precise location data.

🎯 2. How We Use Information

  • To provide GST invoice scanning, extraction and analysis
  • To generate GSTR-1, GSTR-2A and GSTR-3B reports
  • To display your invoice history and analytics dashboard
  • To sync your data across devices via Firebase
  • To send optional notifications (monthly GST summary)
  • To improve AI extraction accuracy
🚫 We do not sell your data to any third parties. We do not use your invoice data for advertising purposes.

🛡️ 3. Data Storage & Security

Where Your Data is Stored

Your invoice data is stored in Google Firebase Firestore, a secure cloud database. Invoice images are stored in Firebase Storage. Both services are hosted on Google Cloud infrastructure with data centres in Asia (asia-south1 region, India).

Security Measures

  • All data transmitted over HTTPS (TLS encryption)
  • Firebase Security Rules enforce that users can only access their own data
  • Invoice images are stored in user-isolated storage paths
  • No raw base64 image data is stored in the database
  • Passwords are managed entirely by Firebase Authentication (we never store passwords)

Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as your account is active. You may delete all your data at any time from Settings → Delete All Data.

🔥 4. Third-Party Services (Firebase)

GST Lens uses the following Google Firebase services:

  • Firebase Authentication — handles secure login (Email/Password, Google Sign-in)
  • Firebase Firestore — stores invoice records and account data
  • Firebase Storage — stores uploaded invoice images and PDFs

Google Firebase is subject to Google's Privacy Policy. GST Lens also uses Google Gemini AI API for invoice OCR assistance. Invoice images sent to Gemini are not retained by Google beyond the API request duration.

Fonts

We use Google Fonts (Syne, DM Sans) which may log font requests. No personal data is passed to Google Fonts.

✅ 5. Your Rights (DPDP Act 2023)

Under India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act 2023, you have the right to:

  • Access — view all data we hold about you (export from Settings)
  • Correction — edit any bill data directly in the app
  • Erasure — delete your account and all data from Settings
  • Portability — export all your data as CSV, Excel or JSON backup
  • Grievance — raise a complaint with our Data Protection Officer
To exercise any of these rights, email us at stratixapp@gmail.com
