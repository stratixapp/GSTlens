📷 2. How Invoices Are Processed

All invoice scanning and data extraction happens entirely on your device, using:

  • Tesseract OCR — open-source, runs locally in your browser, no data sent externally
  • GST Intelligence Engine — built-in rule-based system for GST rate detection and ITC analysis, fully offline
  • PDF.js — local PDF rendering, no server upload required
📄 Invoice images and PDF files are processed in your browser memory only. They are never uploaded to any external AI service, OCR API, or third-party server by GST Lens.

If you enable optional Firebase cloud sync (see Section 3), extracted bill data (not raw images) is synced to your personal Firebase project.

☁️ 3. Optional Cloud Sync (Firebase)

GST Lens offers optional cloud sync using Google Firebase. This is entirely optional — the app works fully offline without it.

If you choose to enable Firebase sync:

  • Your extracted bill data is stored in Firebase Firestore under your user account
  • Invoice images you choose to back up are stored in Firebase Storage under your account
  • Google Firebase is subject to Google's Privacy Policy
  • Stratix Technologies does not have access to your Firebase data — it is stored in your project

You can disconnect Firebase and delete all cloud data at any time from Settings.

🔧 4. How We Use Your Data

We use the data we collect solely to:

  • Provide and maintain the GST Lens Service
  • Authenticate your account and sync data across devices (if Firebase is enabled)
  • Send important Service notifications (e.g., policy changes)
  • Improve the app's OCR accuracy and GST rule engine anonymously
  • Respond to your support requests

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or selling to third parties.

🤝 5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data. Data may be shared only in these limited circumstances:

  • Firebase (Google): For optional cloud sync — only if you enable it
  • Law enforcement: When legally required under Indian law (IT Act 2000, DPDP Act 2023) upon receipt of a valid legal order
  • Business transfer: In the event of a merger or acquisition, users will be notified and the same privacy standards will apply

No data is shared with advertisers, data brokers, analytics companies, or any AI service provider.

🔒 6. Data Security

We take data security seriously:

  • All data in transit is protected by HTTPS/TLS encryption
  • Firebase data is encrypted at rest by Google
  • Invoice images are processed in browser memory and not persisted to any external server by GST Lens
  • Passwords are never stored — authentication uses Firebase Auth (Google/email) with industry-standard security
  • We do not store any payment or financial credentials

🗓️ 7. Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as your account is active:

  • Account data: Retained until you delete your account
  • Bill data (local): Stored on your device; cleared when you clear app data or uninstall
  • Bill data (Firebase): Retained in your Firebase project until you delete it from Settings
  • Account deletion: All data is permanently deleted within 30 days of account deletion

You can delete all your data at any time from Settings → Delete Account.

🇮🇳 8. Your Rights (DPDP Act 2023)

Under the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023, you have the following rights:

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Right to Correction: Request correction of inaccurate personal data
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your personal data
  • Right to Grievance Redressal: Lodge a complaint with us or the Data Protection Board of India
  • Right to Nominate: Nominate another person to exercise these rights on your behalf

To exercise any of these rights, contact our Data Protection Officer at stratixapp@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.

👶 9. Children's Privacy

GST Lens is a business tool intended for use by adults for GST compliance purposes. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 18. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us immediately and we will delete it.

Continued use of the Service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
